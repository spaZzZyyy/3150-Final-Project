Honey Taster Flight

Our honey flights showcase the diversity of forage available across our apiaries, and make great gifts. Each honey flight package is manufactured locally using 100% post-consumer recycled cardboard and printed with a carbon-negative algae based ink. Each flight contains three 5.25oz taster jars and on of our beeswax lip balms.
